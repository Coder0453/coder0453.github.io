About

About us!

As we mentioned before, the podcast was created by Aidan and Sharkey. Their goal? Trying to enlighten people on topics close to their hearts.

The podcast and videos are chaotic, funny and informative all in one, but in the end, it's just all a load of fun. The whole team love being able to spread awareness and positivity by having a catch-up and a laugh on camera.

Each episode is focused on a different life impacting, important topic, which our hosts discuss and give their opinions about. If you need a laugh this is the place to be!
